Sign up for Cultivate Impact®: AKALAKA’s Care Partner Connect

We are excited to extend a special invitation to join your exclusive digital community, Cultivate Impact®: AKALAKA’s Care Partner Connect. This platform has been designed to facilitate collaboration, learning, and resource sharing among teams of care partners like yourself across the state.

As someone with lived experience navigating the wilderness of disability services, your insights are key, and we believe this community will provide a unique opportunity for peer-to-peer support and knowledge exchange.

Once you've joined, we encourage you to introduce yourself, participate in discussions, and share your experiences with fellow care teams. We look forward to seeing you on Care Partner Connect!
